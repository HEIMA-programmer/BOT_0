## What Could Be Improved

### Process Issues
- **US structure was refined mid-sprint**: The backlog reorganization
  (separating Auth into US01) happened after Sprint Planning started.
  Ideally this should be resolved in Sprint 0 / pre-planning.
- **Workload distribution uneven**: Although total capacity was sufficient,
  some team members had concentrated workloads while others had lighter loads.
  Should pay more attention to balanced task distribution during planning.

### Product & Content Issues (Instructor Feedback)
- **Low engagement**: Vocabulary and Listening modules received feedback as "boring" —
  current format lacks interactive or engaging elements to maintain learner interest.
- **Limited audio diversity**: Listening materials use single accent source.
  Need diverse English accents (British, American, Australian, etc.) from multiple
  sources to better prepare students for real academic environments.
- **Missing social features**: No discussion forum or community space for peer
  interaction and collaborative learning.

### Architecture & Scope Issues (Team Discussion)
- **Module disconnection**: Vocabulary, Listening, Speaking, and Discussion modules
  feel isolated — no clear learning progression or cross-module integration.
- **Unclear prioritization**: Current sprint-by-sprint approach completes one module
  fully before starting the next. Consider MVP-first approach to validate overall
  product concept earlier.
- **Academic Culture module**: Not yet prioritized, but could potentially merge
  with discussion/forum features for better cohesion.
