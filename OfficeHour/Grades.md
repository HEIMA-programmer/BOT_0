## Beginner

### Multiple Choice (8)

1. **How does Todd describe the idea of having no grades?**  
   A. As a very competitive system  
   B. As a binary system of achievement  
   C. As a way to earn extra credit  
   D. As a system only for colleges  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Todd says it could be “more binary,” meaning students either achieve or do not achieve.

2. **What kind of school did Nydja attend when she was younger?**  
   A. A school with strict letter grades  
   B. A boarding school  
   C. A school with no grades  
   D. A military school  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** Nydja says she went to a school that “actually didn't have” grades.

3. **How were students organized in that school?**  
   A. By age only  
   B. In one large room by ability  
   C. In separate classes by subject  
   D. Online only  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** She explains that everybody was in “a large room” and teachers worked with students according to their abilities.

4. **How does Nydja say she felt about grades when she was younger?**  
   A. Relaxed  
   B. Confused  
   C. Very stressed  
   D. Uninterested  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** She says directly, “Yes, I was. I was very stressed.”

5. **Until what time did Nydja sometimes stay up to finish projects?**  
   A. Midnight  
   B. One in the morning  
   C. Three or four in the morning  
   D. Sunrise  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** She says she used to stay up until “three or four o'clock in the morning.”

6. **What activity besides studying did Nydja do?**  
   A. Debate  
   B. Track  
   C. Choir  
   D. Drama  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** She says, “I also did track.”

7. **Why does Nydja say students compete with classmates?**  
   A. To become teachers  
   B. To travel abroad  
   C. To get into college and later get a job  
   D. To avoid homework  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** She connects competition with college admission and future jobs.

8. **What kind of parent does Nydja think she would be about grades?**  
   A. Very easygoing  
   B. Not concerned at all  
   C. Strict and demanding  
   D. Unsure  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** She says she would “not be easy on them in that regard at all.”
