### Short Answer (6)

1. **Why does Aiste want to visit Sri Lanka?**  
   **Answer:** Because she likes some Indian culture, but finds Sri Lanka more special and thinks the people there are friendly and outgoing.  
   **Explanation:** Aiste compares Sri Lanka with India and gives cultural and personal reasons.

2. **What are David's reasons for wanting to visit different countries?**  
   **Answer:** He wants to visit Norway for natural resources, the United States for its way of living, and Britain for education.  
   **Explanation:** David gives a different reason for each country.

3. **Why does Katia choose Bangladesh?**  
   **Answer:** She chooses it because she is curious about the country and likes its food.  
   **Explanation:** Those are the two reasons she gives.

4. **What long-term interest leads Mike to want to visit China?**  
   **Answer:** He has been fascinated by Chinese culture, food, and language since he was a boy.  
   **Explanation:** Mike says this interest started when he was young.

5. **What does Jess want to experience in Egypt?**  
   **Answer:** She wants to experience the ancient history, the pyramids, stories about the Nile, and scuba diving.  
   **Explanation:** Jess gives both cultural and activity-based reasons.

6. **Why does Tom choose Brazil?**  
   **Answer:** Because it seems to offer both beaches and mountains, which match his ideal holiday.  
   **Explanation:** Tom says that mix is exactly the kind of vacation he likes.
