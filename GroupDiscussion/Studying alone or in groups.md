### Short Answer (6)

1. **Why does Katia prefer studying alone?**  
   **Answer:** Because she can spend as much time as she needs on one issue.  
   **Explanation:** Katia says studying alone lets her focus on a particular part for as long as necessary.

2. **What advantage of group study does Sunny mention?**  
   **Answer:** Group study gives her new ideas and allows friendly conversation afterward.  
   **Explanation:** Sunny mentions learning new things and having little chit-chats.

3. **Why does Dani reject group study?**  
   **Answer:** Because she wants control over her own learning and thinks groups are distracting.  
   **Explanation:** Dani gives both reasons directly.

4. **What is Shiloh's main argument for studying alone?**  
   **Answer:** She thinks studying alone is more efficient and easier to organize.  
   **Explanation:** Shiloh says groups take longer and she gets less done in the same time.

5. **Why does Phoebe need a group?**  
   **Answer:** She feels a group helps motivate her to study.  
   **Explanation:** Phoebe says she lacks self-motivation when alone.

6. **How does Gareth balance independence and collaboration?**  
   **Answer:** He prefers to study alone but still likes discussing ideas with others.  
   **Explanation:** Gareth values both solo study and collaborative discussion.
