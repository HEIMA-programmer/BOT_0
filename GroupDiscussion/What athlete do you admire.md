# Question Set: What athlete do you admire.txt

## Beginner

### Multiple Choice (8)

1. **Which athlete does Cheryl say she really likes?**  
   A. David Beckham  
   B. Michael Phelps  
   C. Lance Armstrong  
   D. Zlatan Ibrahimovic  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Cheryl says, "His name is Michael Phelps and I really like him."

2. **Why does Jeff admire Michael Jordan?**  
   A. Because he was a swimmer  
   B. Because he balanced family and career  
   C. Because of his tenacity and intensity to win  
   D. Because he did charity work in Africa  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** Jeff says he likes Michael Jordan because of his "tenacity and intensity to win every time he played."

3. **Which athlete does Liz like?**  
   A. Dikembe Mutombo  
   B. Zlatan Ibrahimovic  
   C. Larry Bird  
   D. Michael Phelps  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Liz says, "The famous athlete that I like is Zlatan Ibrahimovic."

4. **Who does Jonathan mention as using his fame and money to do good work in Africa?**  
   A. Michael Jordan  
   B. Lance Armstrong  
   C. Dikembe Mutombo  
   D. David Beckham  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** Jonathan says he respects Dikembe Mutombo because he "used his profile and his money to do a lot of good work in his native Africa."

5. **Which athlete does Hoa say she liked more in the past than now?**  
   A. Michael Phelps  
   B. David Beckham  
   C. Zlatan Ibrahimovic  
   D. Larry Bird  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Hoa says, "I would say I like David Beckham, but I think I like him in the past better than now."

6. **Which sport is Michael Phelps associated with in the text?**  
   A. Football  
   B. Basketball  
   C. Swimming  
   D. Cycling  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** Cheryl says Michael Phelps has broken world records "for swimming."

7. **Why does Jonathan say he does not treat most athletes as role models?**  
   A. He does not know any athletes  
   B. He thinks people respect athletes too much just for sports ability  
   C. He only likes musicians  
   D. He prefers coaches to players  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Jonathan says many people respect athletes too much for their ability to "bounce a ball or hit a ball."

8. **Which athlete does Aiman call his favorite?**  
   A. Lance Armstrong  
   B. Michael Jordan  
   C. David Beckham  
   D. Dikembe Mutombo  
   **Answer:** A  
   **Explanation:** Aiman says, "Lance Armstrong was and still is my favorite."

### Short Answer (6)

1. **Why does Cheryl like Michael Phelps?**  
   **Answer:** She likes him because she thinks he is handsome, very talented, and successful in swimming.  
   **Explanation:** Cheryl gives several reasons, including his looks, talent, and record-breaking success.

2. **What is Jonathan's general view of famous athletes?**  
   **Answer:** He respects their skills, but he does not automatically see them as role models.  
   **Explanation:** Jonathan says he likes their skills but does not respect them more than other public figures.

3. **Why does Liz admire Zlatan Ibrahimovic?**  
   **Answer:** She admires him because he represents success and is a very good football player.  
   **Explanation:** Liz says he "stands for success" and is a really good player.

4. **What does Hoa like about David Beckham besides his football career?**  
   **Answer:** She likes how he manages family life and professional life, and she also mentions his advertising work.  
   **Explanation:** Hoa talks about his big family, work-life balance, and commercials.

5. **Which athlete does Jeff choose, and why?**  
   **Answer:** He chooses Michael Jordan because of Jordan's strong desire to win and be the best.  
   **Explanation:** Jeff focuses on Jordan's competitive spirit.

6. **How does Aiman describe his changing sports interests?**  
   **Answer:** He says that as he changed sports, the athletes he liked also changed.  
   **Explanation:** Aiman mentions different favorites for basketball and cycling.
