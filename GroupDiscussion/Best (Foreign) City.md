## Beginner

### Multiple Choice (8)

1. **Which city does Katia call her favorite?**  
   A. Paris  
   B. Seoul  
   C. New York City  
   D. Singapore  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** Katia says, "I would say New York City is my favorite city."

2. **Why does Tom like Bangkok?**  
   A. Because it is very quiet  
   B. Because it is a good base for day trips and has great food  
   C. Because it is cheap and cold  
   D. Because it has famous art galleries  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Tom says Bangkok is a great place to base yourself, with easy day trips, fantastic weather, and amazing food.

3. **Which city does Lia from Indonesia choose?**  
   A. Barcelona  
   B. Seoul  
   C. Paris  
   D. Singapore  
   **Answer:** D  
   **Explanation:** Lia says her favorite city would be Singapore.

4. **What does Todd say about Barcelona?**  
   A. It has weak culture  
   B. It has great architecture and a strong identity  
   C. It is not by the sea  
   D. It is his least favorite city  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Todd praises Barcelona's architecture, culture, and strong identity.

5. **Which city has Jess visited many weekends for sightseeing and going out?**  
   A. Paris  
   B. Bangkok  
   C. Seoul  
   D. New York  
   **Answer:** A  
   **Explanation:** Jess says she lived near Paris for three months and went there every weekend.

6. **What does Crystal say about the people in Seoul?**  
   A. They are calm and shy  
   B. They are rough, mean, and aggressive  
   C. They are boring  
   D. They are difficult to talk to  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Crystal uses those exact words to describe the people, but says that makes the city fun.

7. **Why does Lia like Singapore?**  
   A. It has mountains and beaches  
   B. It is close to her home country and is beautiful and well organized  
   C. It is famous for clubbing  
   D. It has cheap wine  
   **Answer:** B  
   **Explanation:** Lia says Singapore is close to home, beautiful, clean, and has strict rules.

8. **What is Todd's only major criticism of Barcelona?**  
   A. The people are unfriendly  
   B. The weather is bad  
   C. It can be a little expensive  
   D. There is nothing to do there  
   **Answer:** C  
   **Explanation:** Todd says it is hard to think of anything bad except that it may be a little expensive.

### Short Answer (6)

1. **Why does Katia love New York City?**  
   **Answer:** She loves it because it is lively, full of culture and shows, and gives her a special feeling.  
   **Explanation:** Katia mentions its energy, culture, and emotional effect on her.

2. **What makes Bangkok practical for Tom?**  
   **Answer:** It is a convenient base for day trips and also offers great weather, food, and activity.  
   **Explanation:** Tom emphasizes both convenience and enjoyment.

3. **Why does Lia think Singapore is a very good place?**  
   **Answer:** Because it is beautiful, clean, close to her home country, and people can speak English and Chinese.  
   **Explanation:** Lia gives reasons related to location, order, and communication.

4. **What does Todd admire most about Barcelona?**  
   **Answer:** He admires its architecture, culture, weather, food, and strong identity.  
   **Explanation:** Todd says the city seems to have everything.

5. **Why does Crystal find Seoul fun?**  
   **Answer:** She finds it fun because of the city's intense and aggressive people.  
   **Explanation:** Even though her description sounds negative, she says that energy makes the city exciting.

6. **How does Jess describe a typical enjoyable day in Paris?**  
   **Answer:** Walking around the city, enjoying architecture and galleries, then eating, drinking wine, and going clubbing at night.  
   **Explanation:** Jess describes both daytime sightseeing and nightlife.
